NHL teams

NHL stands for the national hockey league. It has teams in the United States of America and Canada. It is a professional hockey league. The league is the best of the best and it takes time and skill to get into the NHL.

Chicago Blackhawks

The Chicago Blackhawks is an American NHL team. Their home stadium is in Chicago, Illinois. This is my favourite team.

Senators

The Senators is a Canadian NHL team. Their home stadium is here in Ottawa, Ontario!

Lightning

The Tampa Bay Lightning is an American NHL team. Their home stadium is in Tampa, Florida. They won the 2021 Stanley Cup Championship against the Montreal Canadiens.

Maple Leafs

The Maple Leafs is a Canadian NHL team. Their home stadium is in Toronto, Ontario. The last time Toronto won the Stanley Cup was in 1967!
